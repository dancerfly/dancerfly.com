Dancerfly was an event ticketing service for social dances built and run by social dancers. It was created by Harris Lapiroff, Stephen Burrows, and Naomi Morduch Toubman in 2014. Over its nine years of continous operation it was maintained by a number of others and hosted ticketing for over 200 events.

Dancerfly was shut down in August 2023.

It leaves one last gift to the dance community: When we launched Dancerfly, we commissioned artist Lucy Bellwood to create some illustrations of dancers that represented our diverse dance community. In collaboration with Lucy, we're releasing those illustrations under a CC-BY-SA 4.0 license. The files for these illustrations can be found below.
